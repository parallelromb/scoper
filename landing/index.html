How it works

Three steps from client RFP to a SOW you can send.

1

Upload the RFP

Drop the RFP, BRD, SOW template, Jira export, or paste requirements as text. Scoper parses structure, extracts scope items, and detects the delivery domain automatically.

2

Pick a pipeline

Start from a template — Quick, Standard, Enterprise, Re-Estimate, or Jira Import — or drag-and-drop your own pipeline from 28 node types. Load your rate card once; reuse it on every engagement.

3

Send the SOW

Nine agents work in parallel where they can, in sequence where they must. You get a priced SOW: phases, effort hours, rate-carded costs, a schedule, flagged risks, and an audit trail you can defend in a client review.

Built for the agency workflow

Most estimation tools are built for internal engineering teams. Scoper is built for the way agencies and consultancies actually work — RFP in, priced SOW out, with the reasoning you need to defend the number in a client review.

Visual pipeline, not a black box

Drag-and-drop canvas with 28 node types across 6 categories. You see exactly which agents ran, in what order, and why. When a client asks "where did this 240 hours come from?", you can show them.

Runs fully local — client IP stays yours

Ollama + Gemma 3 means no API keys, no cloud, no data leaving your machine. Prospective clients are sending you confidential requirements under NDA. Scoper never exfiltrates them.

Memory with decay, across engagements

Five-layer memory system with Ebbinghaus decay. Estimates learn from your previous SOWs — retention patterns, client-type risks, which scope items always blow out — while forgetting noise. Every engagement sharpens the next.

Your rate card, your agents

Configurable YAML agents let you match your delivery practices. Load your real rate card once, plug in your standard risk library, customize phase names to your methodology. Scoper fits how you scope, not the other way around.
